fs-session-mailer

Receives Fullstory session-summary webhooks and emails them to an address in the payload.

checking…

Endpoint

MethodPathPurpose
POST/webhookReceive session summary & send email
POST/testFire a sample payload (dev only)
GET/healthHealth check

Payload fields

Send a JSON body to POST /webhook. The email address field is required; all others are optional and will be rendered in the email.

FieldRequired?Notes
to_emailone of theseRecipient address — also accepted as email, recipient, recipient_email
session_id—Appears in the email subject and header
session_url—Rendered as a clickable link
user_id—
user_email—
summary—Narrative / AI summary — displayed prominently at the top
duration_seconds—Shown as Xm Ys
page_count—
events—Array of strings or objects — listed below the details table
any other key—Extra scalar fields are appended to the details table

Example payload

{
  "to_email": "alice@example.com",
  "session_id": "abc-123",
  "session_url": "https://app.fullstory.com/ui/ORG/sessions/abc-123",
  "user_id": "user_9876",
  "user_email": "alice@example.com",
  "summary": "User compared pricing tiers and abandoned at checkout.",
  "duration_seconds": 187,
  "page_count": 4,
  "events": [
    "Page view: /pricing",
    "Click: Compare plans",
    "Page view: /checkout",
    "Rage click: Apply coupon",
    "Page exit"
  ]
}
